CPU and GPU Information

Feature iPhone 15 Pro Max Samsung S24 Ultra
CPU Apple A17 Pro (3 nm, hexa-core) Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 (4 nm)
Single-Core Performance 32% higher than S24 Ultra (Geekbench 6) Lower than iPhone 15 Pro Max
GPU Adreno 750 (1 GHz) Apple GPU (6-core graphics)

The iPhone 15 Pro Max, with its Apple A17 Pro chip, excels in single-core performance and efficiency thanks to its advanced 3 nm process and custom high-performance cores. Meanwhile, the Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra, powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 3, offers strong multi-core and GPU performance, particularly in graphics-intensive tasks, due to its 8-core architecture and Adreno 750 GPU. Both devices are top-tier, but they shine in different areas depending on the specific use case.
